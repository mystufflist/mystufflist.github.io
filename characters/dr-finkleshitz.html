Dr. Fredrick Finkleshitz

About

Dr. Fingertits was one of the few people not listed as an escapee to have survived the day of imbalance with minimal harm done to him. After waking up from a coma and seeing the world he once knew all fucked up, he knew that it was his job to fix everything that the Day of Imbalance has caused.

Experiments

Dr. Fingerballs went around capturing the random entities with cages and beartraps set around the main neighborhood. He then took these entities to his lab and experimented on them. His ultimate goal was to reverse the effects of the Day of Imbalance. He unfortunately died after running an experiment on something too dangerous for him. Some speculate that he mistakenly locked eyes with Braxton Jr.
