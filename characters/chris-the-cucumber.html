Chris the Cucumber

Description

Chris the Cucumber is a talking pickle that loved to tell true facts and feed the homeless—but ever since Chef Pee Pee put him into that pickle jar, he hasn't been the same.

Behavior

He tends to hide in the old house waiting for a poor soul there to ask a question.

Occupation

Ripping out eyeballs. It's speculated that he is the one who ripped out Chef Pee Pee's eyes.
